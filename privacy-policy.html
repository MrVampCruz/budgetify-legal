01 — Information

Information We Collect

Budgetify is designed as a local-first, offline application. The vast majority of data you enter never leaves your device.

📱 Data You Provide

Wallet names, transaction details, categories, labels, and any financial data you enter. This is stored exclusively on your device in a local SQLite database.

🔑 Google Account (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we access your Google account ID and name solely to enable Google Drive backup and to identify your subscription with RevenueCat.

📊 Usage & Diagnostics

Anonymous crash reports and performance data may be collected by third-party SDKs (RevenueCat, Google AdMob) to help us improve app stability. This data is never personally identifiable.

🛡️ We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it within the app profile settings.

02 — Usage

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to provide and improve the Budgetify experience. Specifically:

  • To display your wallets, transactions, and financial summaries within the app
  • To calculate balances, budgets, and reports accurately
  • To back up and restore your database via Google Drive (if enabled)
  • To manage your subscription status via RevenueCat
  • To serve relevant ads via Google AdMob (free tier only)
  • To process recurring transactions and send local reminders

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with any third party for marketing purposes.

03 — Storage

Data Storage & Security

All financial data entered into Budgetify is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data does not transit our servers — we have no backend database storing your transactions.

💾 Local Storage

Your SQLite database lives in your device's private app storage. It is not accessible to other apps and is removed when you uninstall Budgetify.

☁️ Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you enable Google Drive backup, your database file is uploaded to the hidden app folder of your personal Google Drive. This folder is invisible to you and other apps — only Budgetify can read it. Google's own privacy policy governs this storage.

⚠️ We strongly recommend enabling Google Drive backup to prevent data loss from device failure or app uninstall.

04 — Google

Google Services

Budgetify integrates with the following Google services, each governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

🔐 Google Sign-In

Used for authentication when enabling Google Drive backup. We only request the minimum permissions needed: your account ID, display name, and Drive app folder access.

📁 Google Drive API

Used exclusively to read and write your database backup file to the hidden app-specific folder. We never access any other files in your Drive.

📢 Google AdMob

Used to display advertisements on the free tier. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. See Section 5 for more details.

05 — Advertising

Advertising

Free tier users may see banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the app free for everyone.

  • AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to serve relevant ads
  • You may see a consent prompt depending on your region (GDPR / US state laws)
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad settings
  • Ads are completely removed for Pro, Lifetime, and Ad-Free subscribers

Budgetify does not directly share any personal or financial data with advertisers. AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Advertising Policy.

06 — Purchases

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Budgetify offers optional paid plans (Pro Monthly, Pro Yearly, Lifetime, Ad-Free) processed through the Google Play Store and managed by RevenueCat.

💳 Payment Processing

All payment transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We never receive or store your payment card information.

🔄 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to track subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat may store your Google account ID as a subscriber identifier. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

07 — Children

Children's Privacy

Budgetify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

08 — Your Rights

Your Rights

Since your data lives on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access — All your data is directly visible within the app
  • Export — Use Google Drive backup to export your database file
  • Delete — Use Settings → Reset App to permanently wipe all local data
  • Portability — Your backup file is a standard SQLite database you can open with any SQLite tool
  • Google Data — Revoke Budgetify's Google Drive access anytime via your Google Account settings

🇪🇺 If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to erasure and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

09 — Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the app.

Continued use of Budgetify after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
